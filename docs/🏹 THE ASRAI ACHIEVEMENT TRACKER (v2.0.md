The Glade Lord's Muster (Hobby & Army Construction)
[ ] In the beginning ...: Play a game of Warhammer using Wood Elves.
[ ] The Deepwood Host: Field a fully painted army of Wood Elves.
[ ] Kick'n it old skool / The good old days: Win a game using the 6th edition book (Bonus points for Glade Riders, Waywatchers, Wardancers, Warhawk Riders, or Sisters of Twilight on a Forest Dragon).
[ ] These boots are made for walking: Field an all-elf, all-infantry army.
[ ] We don't need no stinkin' arrows: Field an army with no archers.
[ ] War! Huh! What is good for?: Field 3 units of Wardancers and 3 units of Warhawk Riders.
[ ] White Death: Field a winter-themed army of Wood Elves with at least one Waystalker in it.
[ ] The Everglades: Only field units with the word 'Glade' in their name.
[ ] The Trees! They're alive!: Field an army of entirely Forest Spirits.
[ ] March of the Ents: Fill your Lords, Special, and Rare slots with Treemen Ancients, Treekin, and Treemen.
[ ] Isn't it grand?: Field a Grand Army of Wood Elves (3000+ points).
[ ] Woodland empire: Win a Mighty Empires campaign using Wood Elves.
[ ] An epic saga: Play a Warhammer narrative campaign using Wood Elves.
[ ] Who's the big brother?: Beat up both High Elves and Dark Elves at least once.

Path of the Arrow (Shooting Phase)
[ ] More dakka: Eliminate an enemy unit using only shooting.
[ ] You thought you had a shooting phase? That's cute: Destroy an enemy warmachine with Glade Guard shooting.
[ ] Master Assassin: Have a Waystalker kill 2+ enemy characters/champions using the Sniper Rule.

[ ] Death from, uh, where was it again?: Kill an enemy character with a Waystalker Sniper shot.
[ ] Legolas, Who?: Have a Waystalker kill a Character/Champion as a stand-and-shoot reaction.
[ ] I am... a stone...: Have a Waystalker survive the entire battle.
[ ] Whoops, wrong arrows!: Fight a battle against an army from the Forces of Order using Starfire Shafts.
[ ] Whoops, wrong arrows again!: Fight a battle against an army from the Forces of Destruction using Moonfire Shot.
[ ] Charmed what?: Remove a Charmed Shield with the Arrow of Kurnous.

Path of the Forest (Magic & Terrain)
[ ] Wrath of the Forest: Wipe out a unit with Tree Singing.
[ ] My turf!: Use 'Awakening of the Woods' on a unit in your Free Forest.
[ ] No! Bad Forest!: Use Tree Singing on a Blood Forest to walk it back to the enemy.
[ ] Blood for the ... tree god?: Make your free forest a Blood Forest.
[ ] The Trees are Alive! / And I Thought the Forests Were Our Friends: Deploy all your selected woods as Wild Woods.
[ ] Another wrong turn!: Play on a battlefield with no forests (not even the free one!).
[ ] Homesick: Play a battle on a field with at least six forests.
[ ] Keep off the grass!: Drive an enemy unit out of a wood.
[ ] Wrong turn!: Use Moonstone of the Hidden Ways to reappear in the same forest.
[ ] You wont like me when I'm angry: Engage an enemy unit after casting Okkam's Mindrazer and Flesh to Stone on the same unit.
[ ] What the Crap type of Steed is that?: Cast Steed of Shadows on a Tree Man Ancient and move the full 20 inches.
[ ] Warp Trees Ahead Captain!: Cast Timewarp (or Birona's Timewarp) on a Treeman or Treeman Ancient and move the full 20 inches.
[ ] Neener Neener Neener: Reduce an enemy unit to a -3 to hit modifier with Ice Shard Blizzard, Glittering Scales, and Curse of Anraheir.
[ ] Whoops! Can we start again?: Lose your wizard to Dimensional Cascade on Turn 1.
[ ] Power overwhelming!: Have your wizard die due to casting 'Power of Darkness'.
[ ] May the Odds be Ever in your Favor: Destroy an opposing model using a spell or effect that uses a Characteristic test, but would only fail on a 6.
[ ] Charmed Ones: Use a Spellsinger, a Shadowdancer (with magic), and a Branchwraith (with magic) in the same battle.
[ ] Light as a feather, stiff as a board: Use two Spellweavers in a battle.
[ ] Nero Burning Loren: Play a game using only the Lore of Fire.
[ ] Heavy metal: Play a game using only the Lore of Metal.
